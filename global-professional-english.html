Common challenges

Common problems this coaching can help with

Global meetings

You may join meetings in English but hesitate to speak, interrupt, clarify, or challenge ideas.

英語会議に参加していても、発言・確認・反論・質問が難しいことがあります。

Overseas assignment readiness

Before 海外赴任 or 海外勤務, you may need to prepare for real workplace communication, not just travel English.

海外赴任・海外勤務前には、旅行英語ではなく仕事で使う英語が必要です。

Cross-cultural clarity

What sounds natural in Japanese business communication may feel too indirect or unclear in global teams.

日本語では自然な伝え方が、海外チームには遠回しすぎることがあります。

Leadership presence

Global roles require calm, clear, and credible communication under pressure.

グローバルな役割では、プレッシャーの中でも落ち着いて明確に伝える力が必要です。

How coaching works

Practice is targeted, practical, and connected to your real goal.

This is not random conversation practice. We identify the situations where English matters most, then practice the language, structure, delivery, and confidence needed for those situations.

ただ英会話をするのではなく、必要な場面を明確にして、その場面で本当に使う英語・構成・話し方・自信を練習します。

  • Prepare for English meetings and stakeholder discussions
  • Practice concise updates, recommendations, and Q&A
  • Develop communication confidence for global teams
  • Clarify cultural and professional communication expectations
  • Train practical language for overseas work situations
