Common challenges

Overseas assignments

You may need English for meetings, reporting, leadership, and relationship building soon after relocation.

海外赴任・海外勤務では、現地での会議、報告、関係構築の英語が必要になります。

Global meetings

You need to speak clearly even when the speed, accents, and expectations are different.

英語会議では、スピード、発音、文化が違っても明確に発言する力が必要です。

Stakeholder communication

Your message must be clear to people with different roles, cultures, and priorities.

立場や文化の違う相手にも、目的と要点が伝わる説明が必要です。

Career advancement

Communication affects visibility, trust, promotion, and leadership opportunities.

英語での発信力は、評価、信頼、昇進、リーダーシップ機会に関わります。

FAQ / よくある質問

Do I need advanced English?

No. Coaching is adjusted to your current level, role, goals, and professional situation. 英語が完璧でなくても大丈夫です。

Can you help with English presentations?

Yes. 英語プレゼン, Q&A, verbal delivery, structure, examples, and executive presence are core training areas.

Do you work with Japanese professionals overseas?

Yes. Coaching is available online for professionals in Japan, overseas, and global teams.

Is this business English?

It includes business English, but the focus is broader: communication performance, confidence, clarity, authority, and credibility.

Can you help with English meetings?

Yes. We practice 英語会議, meeting participation, stakeholder communication, concise answers, and discussion performance.

Can you help before an overseas assignment?

Yes. 海外勤務 and 海外赴任 preparation can include meetings, presentations, self-introductions, small talk, and Q&A.

Do you work with MBA students?

Yes. MBA students can practice presentations, case discussions, interviews, networking, and class participation in English.

What happens in the free consultation?

We discuss your goals, current communication challenges, timeline, and whether coaching is a good fit.

Is this for grammar correction?

Grammar correction is included when needed, but the main focus is communicating clearly and effectively under pressure.

Can lessons be customized?

Yes. Sessions are built around your real work, deadlines, presentations, interviews, meetings, and communication goals.

Can you help with global teams?

Yes. We train concise updates, meeting participation, stakeholder communication, and cross-cultural communication.
