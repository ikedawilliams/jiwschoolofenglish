Common challenges

Common problems this coaching can help with

Main point arrives too late

The audience waits too long to understand your conclusion, recommendation, or purpose.

結論や提案が遅く、聞き手が何を聞けばいいのかわかりにくくなります。

Weak verbal delivery

Your voice, pacing, pauses, and emphasis may not match the importance of your message.

声、間、スピード、強調がメッセージの重要性と合わないことがあります。

Difficult Q&A

Even strong presentations can lose authority during unexpected questions.

発表が良くても、想定外の質問で自信が弱く見えることがあります。

Too much detail

Detailed explanations may hide the message instead of strengthening it.

説明が多すぎると、かえって重要なポイントが埋もれてしまいます。

How coaching works

Practice is targeted, practical, and connected to your real goal.

This is not random conversation practice. We identify the situations where English matters most, then practice the language, structure, delivery, and confidence needed for those situations.

ただ英会話をするのではなく、必要な場面を明確にして、その場面で本当に使う英語・構成・話し方・自信を練習します。

  • Design clear opening messages and conclusions
  • Practice concise explanation and supporting examples
  • Improve voice, pacing, pausing, and emphasis
  • Prepare for Q&A and pressure moments
  • Build audience-centered presentation flow

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Can you help with a real presentation?

Yes. We can use your actual slides, topic, or meeting situation.

はい。実際のスライド、テーマ、会議内容を使って練習できます。

Can we practice Q&A?

Yes. Q&A practice is one of the most important parts of presentation coaching.

はい。Q&A練習は英語プレゼン対策で非常に重要です。

Do you correct pronunciation?

Yes, especially when pronunciation affects clarity, confidence, or listener understanding.

はい。伝わりやすさや自信に影響する発音を中心に修正します。

Can this help with MBA presentations?

Yes. MBA presentations, case reports, and class discussions are supported.

はい。MBAでの発表、ケース発表、クラス内発言にも対応できます。

Do I need perfect grammar?

No. We focus on clarity, structure, delivery, and confidence. Grammar is corrected when it affects communication.

いいえ。文法は大切ですが、明確さ、構成、話し方、自信を重視します。

Can I prepare in a short time?

Yes. We can focus on the highest-impact parts first: opening, conclusion, key message, and Q&A.

はい。時間が短い場合は、冒頭、結論、主張、Q&Aなど優先度の高い部分から練習します。
