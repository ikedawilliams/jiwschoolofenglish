How Lessons Work

Speak a lot. Get corrected. Try again.

Lessons are not passive explanation time. You speak, fix mistakes, and practice again until the language becomes usable.

1

Warm-up

We start with natural conversation connected to your real life.

2

Correction

Grammar, pronunciation, vocabulary, and phrasing are corrected directly.

3

Role Play

We practice realistic interviews, travel, presentations, or trouble situations.

4

Next Focus

You leave knowing what improved and what to practice next.
