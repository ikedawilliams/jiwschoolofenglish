Common communication challenges

Verbal delivery

Your ideas may be strong, but your delivery may sound hesitant, flat, rushed, or unclear under pressure.

考えはあるのに、英語になると声・間・スピード・言い方が弱く見えることがあります。

Communication confidence

You may understand the topic, but lose confidence when speaking to senior stakeholders or international colleagues.

内容は理解していても、上司・海外チーム・顧客の前では自信が下がることがあります。

Structure and clarity

The listener may understand your words, but not quickly understand your main point, reasoning, or recommendation.

単語は伝わっても、「結局何が言いたいのか」がすぐに伝わらないことがあります。

Authority under pressure

In meetings, Q&A, and presentations, you may sound less decisive than your actual expertise.

会議やQ&Aで、本当の実力よりも自信がなさそうに聞こえてしまうことがあります。

How coaching works

This is not casual conversation practice. Training focuses on the communication behaviors that affect credibility, leadership, and professional influence.

  • Clarify the main message before adding detail
  • Practice structured responses for meetings and Q&A
  • Improve verbal delivery, pacing, and confidence
  • Use examples and evidence more effectively
  • Develop executive presence in English
