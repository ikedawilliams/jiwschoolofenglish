Who this is for

For learners and professionals with a real reason to improve.

  • Japanese professionals working in global business environments
  • Managers preparing for English meetings, presentations, and Q&A
  • Professionals on overseas assignments or working abroad
  • MBA students and future leaders
  • Professionals who feel less clear or confident in English than in Japanese

Common challenges

Common problems this coaching can help with

Verbal delivery

Your ideas may be strong, but your delivery may sound hesitant, flat, rushed, or unclear under pressure.

考えはあるのに、英語になると声・間・スピード・言い方が弱く見えることがあります。

Communication confidence

You may understand the topic, but lose confidence when speaking to senior stakeholders or international colleagues.

内容は理解していても、上司・海外チーム・顧客の前では自信が下がることがあります。

Structure and clarity

The listener may understand your words, but not quickly understand your main point, reasoning, or recommendation.

単語は伝わっても、「結局何が言いたいのか」がすぐに伝わらないことがあります。

Authority under pressure

In meetings, Q&A, and presentations, you may sound less decisive than your actual expertise.

会議やQ&Aで、本当の実力よりも自信がなさそうに聞こえてしまうことがあります。

How coaching works

Practice is targeted, practical, and connected to your real goal.

This is not random conversation practice. We identify the situations where English matters most, then practice the language, structure, delivery, and confidence needed for those situations.

ただ英会話をするのではなく、必要な場面を明確にして、その場面で本当に使う英語・構成・話し方・自信を練習します。

  • Clarify the main message before adding detail
  • Practice structured responses for meetings and Q&A
  • Improve verbal delivery, pacing, and confidence
  • Use examples and evidence more effectively
  • Develop executive presence in English

Yes. Presentations, Q&A, stakeholder communication, and verbal delivery are core areas.

はい。英語プレゼン、Q&A、ステークホルダーへの説明、話し方を練習します。

Do you work with Japanese professionals overseas?

Yes. Coaching is available online for professionals in Japan and abroad.

Yes. We practice short answers, structured opinions, questions, clarification, and confident participation.
