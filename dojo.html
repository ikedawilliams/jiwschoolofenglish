How to use the Dojo

To get highly tailored practice, first tell the AI who you are and what you want.

  • Part 1 sets up you
  • Part 2 is the training task

PART 1 — Identity Prompt

My name is (name).

I am a (job / student / role).

I (work / study) at (company / school).

I use English (in meetings / on exams / with customers / etc.).

My current level is (beginner / intermediate / advanced).

Please adjust your English level, examples, corrections, and speed to suit me.

I want to do this (verbally / via text).

If you need more information, ask me before continuing.

PART 2 — DOJO TRAINING PROMPTS

(Use after sending Part 1)
Choose one area. Train it. Stop.

Vocabulary

Please assess my English level.

Focus on my spelling and basic word usage.

Task format:
Give me a Japanese word (in Japanese).
I will write the English word.

After I write the English word:
- check my spelling
- correct it if needed
- briefly explain the mistake (if there is one)

Then ask me to make ONE original English sentence using the word.

After my sentence:
- correct spelling and grammar
- suggest a more natural version if needed
- keep explanations short and simple

Use common, practical words that I would use in daily life or work.

Prepare 10 words in total.
Go one word at a time.

At the end:
- give brief overall feedback
- tell me my current level
- ask if I want to increase or decrease the difficulty

Grammar

Please assess my English level.

Focus on my grammar skills.

Important: Ask the questions one by one (not a list of 10).
Wait for my answer each time before giving the next task.

Goal: Test my ability to:
- use verb tenses correctly
- form natural sentences
- connect ideas clearly

Order (easy → harder):
Start with short, easy tasks like:
- fill in the blank (choose the correct word form)
- sentence word order (rearrange words)
- choose the correct verb tense / form

Then move to medium tasks like:
- change a sentence to past / future
- make a correct question

Finish with harder tasks like:
- join sentences with and / but / because
- write 2–3 sentences about a topic (short writing)

Total: Prepare about 10 short questions or tasks.
Keep instructions simple and clear.
If I make a mistake, correct it briefly and show the natural version.

At the end:
- give brief overall feedback
- tell me my current level
- ask if I want to increase or decrease the difficulty

Listening

Please help me improve my listening skills using AI-generated audio.

I am using this on my phone in voice mode.

First, read a short listening script aloud.
Do not show the script yet.

Script requirements:
- Topic: (choose the situation)
- Number of speakers: (1 / 2 / 3)
- Accent: (aim for the requested accent)
- Speed: (slow / natural / fast)
- Length: (30 seconds / 1 minute / 2 minutes)

Important rules:
- Read the script once only at first.
- Do not explain or summarize before I listen.

After I listen:
- ask simple comprehension questions
- check my understanding (you know the script)
- ask me to answer verbally

Then:
- read the script a second time
- ask me to notice missed words, sounds, or meaning

After that:
- show me the full script
- point out difficult parts (linking, weak sounds, reductions, speed)
- explain briefly why they were hard

Finally:
- suggest one short follow-up exercise
- ask if I want an easier or harder version
- optionally change speed, accent, or length for the next round

Pronunciation

Please assess my English level.

Focus on my pronunciation.

Pay special attention to:
- difficult sounds (for example R / L)
- word stress
- sentence rhythm and intonation

Give me short pronunciation tasks and examples.

Ask me to repeat or read aloud.

Prepare several short exercises.

At the end:
- give brief feedback
- tell me my current level
- ask if I want to increase or decrease the difficulty

Writing

Please assess my English level.

Focus on my writing skills.

Ask me to write a short text, such as:
- a diary entry
- an email
- a short paragraph about a topic

Test my ability to:
- write clear sentences
- use natural grammar and vocabulary
- organize ideas logically

Prepare several short writing tasks.

At the end:
- give brief feedback
- tell me my current level
- ask if I want to increase or decrease the difficulty

Role Plays

Please assess my English level.

Focus on my ability to communicate in real-life situations.

Create a realistic role play based on my needs.

Test my ability to:
- respond naturally
- handle follow-up questions
- keep the conversation going

Guide the role play step by step.

At the end:
- give brief feedback
- tell me my current level
- ask if I want to increase or decrease the difficulty
