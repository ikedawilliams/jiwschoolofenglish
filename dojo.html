How to use the Dojo

To get highly tailored practice, first tell the AI who you are and what you want.

  • Part 1 sets up you
  • Part 2 is the training task

You can reuse Part 1 every time.

PART 1 — Identity Prompt

(Copy & paste first)

My name is (David).

I am a (job / student / role).

I work or study at (company / school).

I use English (for / with) (meetings / travel / presentations / exams / patients).

My current level is (beginner / intermediate / advanced).

Please adjust your English level, examples, corrections, and speed to suit me.

I want to do this (verbally / via text).

If you need more information, ask me before continuing.

This is Part 1 of my prompt. I will send Part 2 next.

PART 2 — DOJO TRAINING PROMPTS

(Use after sending Part 1)
Choose one area. Train it. Stop.

Spelling

Please assess my English level.

Focus on my spelling skills.

Test my ability to spell common and practical English words.

Use these task types:
- dictation
- fill in the blank
- correct the spelling

Prepare 10 questions.

At the end:
- give brief feedback
- tell me my current level
- ask if I want to increase or decrease the difficulty

Vocabulary

Please assess my English level.

Focus on my vocabulary skills.

Ask me to explain the meaning of English words and phrases.

Use this quiz style:
- true / false
- multiple choice
- fill in the blank

Prepare 10 questions.

At the end:
- give brief feedback
- tell me my current level
- ask if I want to increase or decrease the difficulty

Grammar

Please assess my English level.

Focus on my grammar skills.

Ask me to answer questions in English or write / talk about a topic.

Test my ability to:
- use verb tenses correctly
- form natural sentences
- connect ideas clearly

Prepare about 10 questions or short tasks.

At the end:
- give brief feedback
- tell me my current level
- ask if I want to increase or decrease the difficulty

Pronunciation (Sounds & Rhythm)

Please assess my English level.

Focus on my pronunciation.

Pay special attention to:
- difficult sounds (for example R / L)
- word stress
- sentence rhythm and intonation

Give me short pronunciation tasks and examples.

Ask me to repeat or read aloud.

Prepare several short exercises.

At the end:
- give brief feedback
- tell me my current level
- ask if I want to increase or decrease the difficulty

Writing (Diaries, Emails, Short Texts)

Please assess my English level.

Focus on my writing skills.

Ask me to write a short text, such as:
- a diary entry
- an email
- a short paragraph about a topic

Test my ability to:
- write clear sentences
- use natural grammar and vocabulary
- organize ideas logically

Prepare several short writing tasks.

At the end:
- give brief feedback
- tell me my current level
- ask if I want to increase or decrease the difficulty

Role Plays

Please assess my English level.

Focus on my ability to communicate in real-life situations.

Create a realistic role play based on my needs.

Test my ability to:
- respond naturally
- handle follow-up questions
- keep the conversation going

Guide the role play step by step.

At the end:
- give brief feedback
- tell me my current level
- ask if I want to increase or decrease the difficulty
