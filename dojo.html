AI Dojo

恥ずかしくない。
何度でも練習できる。

レッスンの間も、英語は伸ばせます。下のプロンプトをコピーして、ChatGPTの音声モードやテキストで練習してください。

1

Part 1

最初に、自分の名前・レベル・目的をAIに伝えます。

2

Part 2

練習したいメニューを1つ選んでコピーします。

3

Stop

一度に全部やらない。1メニューだけで十分です。

Part 1

最初に送る自己設定

黒いカッコの部分だけ、自分用に変えてください。

AI Prompt

自己設定Identity Prompt

My name is (name).
I am a Japanese person studying English.
My current level is (beginner / lower intermediate / intermediate / upper intermediate / advanced / not sure).
I want to improve (travel / school / business / speaking confidence / etc.).
Please adjust your English level, examples, corrections, and speed to suit me.
I want to practice (verbally / by text).
If you need more information, ask me before continuing.
Please respond with: OK, I am ready for Part 2.

Part 2

1つ選んで、練習する。

コピーしてChatGPTに貼り付けてください。音声練習なら、先にVoice ModeをONにします。

AI Prompt

ロールプレイRole Play

ROLE PLAY — VOICE MODE

I am using ChatGPT in voice mode for speaking practice.
Start the role play immediately using the settings below.

Situation: (travel / restaurant / interview / school / work / daily life)
My role: (customer / student / traveler / employee / etc.)
Speed: (slow / natural)
Level: (beginner / intermediate / advanced)

Rules:
- Stay in character.
- Speak in short, natural turns.
- After my response, give one short correction only if needed.
- Then continue the conversation.
- If I do not understand, help me ask for repetition naturally.

Start now. Set the scene briefly and ask me the first question.

AI Prompt

リスニングListening

LISTENING TRAINING — VOICE MODE

Create a short listening activity for my English level.

Topic: (travel / school / daily life / work / interview)
Speed: (slow / natural)
Length: (30 seconds / 1 minute)

Rules:
- Read the script aloud once.
- Do not show the script before the first listen.
- Ask me 3 comprehension questions.
- Then read it again.
- Finally show the script and explain difficult words briefly.

AI Prompt

発音Pronunciation

PRONUNCIATION TRAINING — VOICE MODE

Help me practice natural English pronunciation.

Focus on:
- clarity
- rhythm
- stress
- intonation

Give me one short sentence at a time.
Say it first.
Ask me to repeat.
Give one correction only.
Then ask me to repeat again.

AI Prompt

ライティングWriting

WRITING TRAINING

Please assess my English writing level.
Give me one short writing task at a time.

Choose one task:
1 Diary
2 Email
3 Opinion
4 EIKEN-style answer

After I write:
- correct my mistakes
- show a more natural version
- give 3 short comments
- ask me to rewrite one sentence
