Who this is for

For learners and professionals with a real reason to improve.

英語を「なんとなく勉強する」のではなく、仕事・留学・旅行・面接・海外生活など、具体的な目的のために練習したい方のためのページです。

  • Japanese professionals who feel less confident in English
  • People who freeze or hesitate in English meetings
  • Professionals preparing for interviews, presentations, or overseas work
  • MBA students who need stronger class participation
  • Learners who understand English but struggle to speak under pressure

Common challenges

Common problems this coaching can help with

You know the answer but hesitate

You may understand the topic but wait too long, speak too softly, or avoid giving a clear opinion.

内容はわかっているのに、発言が遅れたり、声が小さくなったり、はっきり言えないことがあります。

You sound less capable than you are

Your English may make you appear less confident or less senior than your actual ability.

英語になると、本来の実力よりも自信がないように見えてしまうことがあります。

Pressure changes your speech

Under pressure, sentences become longer, less organized, or harder to finish.

緊張すると、文が長くなったり、まとまりにくくなったりします。

You avoid opportunities

You may avoid speaking, presenting, or volunteering because English feels risky.

英語が不安で、発表や発言の機会を避けてしまうことがあります。

How coaching works

Practice is targeted, practical, and connected to your real goal.

This is not random conversation practice. We identify the situations where English matters most, then practice the language, structure, delivery, and confidence needed for those situations.

ただ英会話をするのではなく、必要な場面を明確にして、その場面で本当に使う英語・構成・話し方・自信を練習します。

  • Practice short, clear answers under pressure
  • Build reliable response patterns for meetings and Q&A
  • Improve verbal delivery and pacing
  • Train confidence through repetition and feedback
  • Replace avoidance with controlled participation

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

英語に自信がなくても大丈夫ですか？

Yes. This page is specifically for people who feel less confident in English than in Japanese.

はい。英語に自信がない方こそ対象です。

Can confidence really improve?

Yes. Confidence improves when you have repeatable patterns, correction, and pressure practice.

はい。使える型、修正、実践練習を重ねることで改善できます。

Is this therapy?

No. This is communication performance coaching, not therapy.

いいえ。これは心理カウンセリングではなく、英語コミュニケーションの実践コーチングです。

Can this help with meetings?

Yes. We practice speaking earlier, answering clearly, asking questions, and confirming understanding.

はい。会議で早めに発言する、明確に答える、質問する、確認する練習をします。

Can beginners join?

Yes, if they have a specific goal. The level and speed are adjusted.

はい。具体的な目標があれば、レベルとスピードを調整して進めます。

How is this different from regular English conversation?

The goal is not chatting. The goal is to speak more clearly and confidently when it matters.

雑談ではなく、大事な場面でより明確に、自信を持って話すことが目的です。
