Common challenges

Common problems this coaching can help with

Audition interviews

You may need to explain your background, goals, training, strengths, and motivation in English.

オーディションでは、経歴、目標、練習歴、強み、志望理由を英語で説明する必要があります。

First impressions

Small talk, greetings, and polite questions affect how confident and professional you appear.

挨拶、雑談、丁寧な質問は第一印象に影響します。

Dinner or social situations

Auditions are not only stage performance. You may need to build relationships in English.

オーディションは舞台だけではありません。食事や会話で関係を作る場面もあります。

Pressure and nerves

Even prepared students may freeze when the situation feels real.

準備していても、本番に近い場面では緊張して言葉が出ないことがあります。

How coaching works

Practice is targeted, practical, and connected to your real goal.

This is not random conversation practice. We identify the situations where English matters most, then practice the language, structure, delivery, and confidence needed for those situations.

ただ英会話をするのではなく、必要な場面を明確にして、その場面で本当に使う英語・構成・話し方・自信を練習します。

  • Practice self-introductions and audition interview answers
  • Prepare questions for teachers, directors, and company staff
  • Role-play dinner, travel, and first-meeting situations
  • Correct grammar that affects clarity and confidence
  • Use AI Dojo to repeat practice between lessons
