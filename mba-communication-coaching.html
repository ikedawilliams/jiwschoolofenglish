Common challenges

Common problems this coaching can help with

Case discussions

You may understand the case, but struggle to express your position quickly and clearly in English.

ケースの内容は理解していても、英語で意見をすばやく明確に言うのが難しいことがあります。

MBA presentations

You need to sound structured, confident, and credible when presenting business ideas in English.

英語で発表するとき、構成・自信・説得力が必要になります。

Interview preparation

MBA interviews require more than correct English. They require a clear story, motivation, and future direction.

MBA面接では、正しい英語だけでなく、経験・志望理由・将来像を明確に伝える力が必要です。

Networking confidence

You may need to introduce yourself, ask questions, and build relationships naturally in English.

自己紹介、質問、雑談、関係作りを英語で自然に行う練習が必要です。

How coaching works

Practice is targeted, practical, and connected to your real goal.

This is not random conversation practice. We identify the situations where English matters most, then practice the language, structure, delivery, and confidence needed for those situations.

ただ英会話をするのではなく、必要な場面を明確にして、その場面で本当に使う英語・構成・話し方・自信を練習します。

  • Build clear Position → Reason → Example answers
  • Practice MBA case discussion language
  • Prepare interview stories and leadership examples
  • Improve class participation and discussion confidence
  • Train concise answers under pressure

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this only for current MBA students?

No. It is also useful for MBA applicants, future MBA students, and professionals considering global leadership roles.

いいえ。MBA受験者、MBA志望者、将来グローバルに活躍したい社会人にも対応しています。

Can you help with GLOBIS MBA English?

Yes. We can practice case discussion, presentations, and professional English communication related to MBA study.

はい。グロービスMBAなどで必要なケースディスカッション、発表、英語での意見交換を練習できます。

Can we practice MBA interviews?

Yes. We can prepare answers about your background, motivation, leadership, career goals, and why MBA.

はい。経歴、志望理由、リーダーシップ経験、キャリア目標などの回答を練習できます。

Can beginners do this?

Yes, but the training is goal-based. We adjust vocabulary, speed, and difficulty to your level.

はい。レベルに合わせて語彙、スピード、難易度を調整します。

Do you correct grammar?

Yes, but grammar correction is connected to communication performance, not just textbook accuracy.

はい。ただし、文法だけでなく、相手に伝わるかどうかを重視します。

Can this help with overseas MBA study?

Yes. We can practice speaking, presentations, class participation, and networking before or during your program.

はい。海外MBA留学前・留学中の発言、発表、会話、ネットワーキング練習に対応できます。
