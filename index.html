About

Hi, I’m John Ikeda-Williams, the founder of JIW School of English.

I’ve been teaching English in Japan for over 25 years, working with students ranging from junior high school students to business professionals.

My focus is simple: helping Japanese learners speak clear, natural English with confidence in real situations.

The 4K Method

JIW School of English uses the 4K Method, inspired by martial arts training.

Before lessons

  • Kihon – Grammar, vocabulary, pronunciation (input)
  • Kata – Diary writing, pattern practice, AI role-plays (output)

During lessons

  • Kumite – Live conversation and practical speaking
  • Kachi – Confidence and real-world results

What lessons feel like

  • Plenty of speaking time
  • Clear, direct corrections
  • A relaxed but focused atmosphere
  • Mistakes are expected — and useful
  • Detailed feedback after lessons
